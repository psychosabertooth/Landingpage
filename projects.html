Archives

A detailed description of the projects I have worked on till now as a part of curriculum and out of personal interest

Arduino Pedometer Embark on a journey into the world of fitness and technology with my Arduino Pedometer project. This innovative device leverages the power of Arduino to accurately track steps, distance, and calories burned. By integrating a motion sensor and real-time data processing, this pedometer provides users with precise activity metrics, helping them to achieve their fitness goals. Its compact design and user-friendly interface make it a perfect companion for daily walks or intense workouts, proving that staying healthy can be both simple and fun.

RFID Bus Ticketing System Revolutionize public transportation with my RFID Bus Ticketing System, designed to streamline the ticketing process and enhance commuter convenience. This system employs RFID technology to enable contactless fare collection, reducing the time and hassle associated with traditional ticketing methods. By simply tapping their RFID-enabled cards, passengers can effortlessly board buses, while the system simultaneously tracks journey data and ensures secure transactions. This project not only improves the efficiency of public transport but also paves the way for a smarter, more connected urban mobility experience.

Driver Drowsiness Detection Safety meets innovation in my Driver Drowsiness Detection project, aimed at preventing road accidents caused by fatigued driving. Utilizing advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms, this system continuously monitors the driver's facial features and eye movements to detect signs of drowsiness. When signs of fatigue are identified, the system promptly alerts the driver with visual and auditory warnings, urging them to take a break. This project underscores my commitment to leveraging technology for enhancing road safety and saving lives.

Yoga Pose Detection Combine the ancient practice of yoga with modern technology through my Yoga Pose Detection project. This system uses computer vision to analyze and recognize various yoga poses, offering real-time feedback and corrections to practitioners. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced yogi seeking to perfect your form, this project provides an interactive and personalized yoga experience. By blending tradition with innovation, it promotes physical well-being and mindfulness in the digital age.

Dashboard for Storytelling Analysis Unleash the power of data visualization with my Dashboard for Storytelling Analysis. This project transforms complex datasets into compelling narratives, enabling users to uncover insights and make informed decisions. Featuring interactive charts, graphs, and visual elements, the dashboard is designed to present data in an engaging and understandable manner. Whether used for business intelligence, academic research, or media production, this project demonstrates my ability to turn data into stories that captivate and inform.
